The Brown Bear

The brown bear is a large bear species found across Eurasia and North America.

A hyperlink to: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brown_bear

It is a member of the family Ursidae, which includes other bear species such as the polar bear and the black bear.

The brown bear is known for its distinctive hump on its back, which is made up of muscle and fat.

It has a thick coat of fur that can vary in color from light brown to dark brown, and it has long claws that are used for digging and climbing.
